Stucher

Stucher (Student & Staff Information System) App

Team mini-project

  • Problem Statement -
    Development of an application for storing & retrieving data of students & staff and displaying the data whenever requested.
  • Platform - Android Studio.
  • Language – Java.
  • Database – MySQL.
  • Description -
    The act of collecting & storing the details of staff & students is a tedious job. As a resukt the Professors/office staff have to see to it that the data entry is done by each & every student correctly & that to with valid details. To make it easy for Professors/ office staff we have developed an application named 'Stucher', which facilitates easy data filling & retrieval as well maintainig the records. The application itsel checks for unique entries. This ultimately reduces the manual work of keeping the records.
    Users of the system - Principal, Staff (teaching & non-teaching) & Students.
    In this application registration is done by all three users (ie, Principal, Staff mmvers & Students ). But account is only created of Principal & staff members. Students just register them for storing their details. Login is done only by Principal & staff members. After entering the username & password, if its valid they go in thier account. An authentication is provided for Principal. If the authenticated password is correct then only Principal is logged in. After successful sign in, staff members can view students details whereas Principal can view students as well staff members details.
